# Scenarios

**TeamMind is built for any team where knowledge-intensive collaboration is core to the work.**

## 🏢 The Department Brain

**Best for**: Marketing, R&D, HR, Operations — any enterprise team

Build a dedicated AI workgroup for each department. Upload internal documents,
past projects, and working standards. AI becomes the first guide for new hires
and a daily collaborator for experienced staff.

- New employees gain access to the full depth of department knowledge from day one — onboarding time drops significantly
- Discussions, decisions, and project rationale are automatically preserved — no longer dependent on individual memory
- Strategy research, competitive analysis, and campaign drafts — AI participates throughout and keeps a complete record

## ⚖️ Professional Services — Law, Consulting, Therapy

**Best for**: Law firms, management consultancies, mental health practices

Create a private, isolated workgroup for each client. AI manages case files,
communication history, and project progress — all in one place.

- Client data is fully isolated across groups, with strong security guarantees
- AI recalls case details and past communications instantly, eliminating repetitive briefings
- On-premise deployment available to meet industry compliance and confidentiality requirements

## 🎓 Education & Corporate Training

**Best for**: Schools, training organizations, enterprise L&D teams

Build a Classroom Hub — upload course materials, lecture notes, and practice sets
directly into the AI knowledge base.

- Learners get accurate, curriculum-grounded answers from AI around the clock
- Key Points help learners quickly grasp the core content without reading everything
- Course knowledge accumulates over time, creating a continuously improving teaching knowledge base

## 🎨 Creative Studios — Design, Strategy, Content

**Best for**: Ad agencies, design teams, content creation studios

Set up a project workgroup, upload brand assets, references, and design guidelines.
AI joins every brainstorming session from the start.

- Mixed-layout documents — brand books, visual identity guidelines — are fully understood by AI, with accurate visual context
- Every idea and discussion is preserved in full, surviving team turnover and project transitions
- Multiple project groups run in parallel, with assets and creative directions kept completely separate

## 📈 Investment Research & Financial Analysis

**Best for**: Investment firms, research departments, analyst teams

Load market reports, financial data, and research literature directly into the group knowledge base.
The whole team shares one AI analyst with deep, up-to-date domain knowledge.

- Upload the latest research reports and AI updates its knowledge immediately
- Analysts query and get synthesized conclusions fast — research cycles shrink significantly
- Team discussions and analytical conclusions are automatically preserved, building an institutional knowledge asset over time

## 🌐 Website Community Growth — SiteMind™

**Best for**: SaaS products, open-source projects, content platforms, e-commerce brands

With SiteMind™, every website visitor becomes a potential community member.

**The three-step conversion flow:**

1. **Embed the entry point** — Add the SiteMind™ component to any page of your site, no architectural changes required
2. **AI-guided conversation** — Visitors talk to AI, which answers product questions accurately via RAG
3. **One-click join** — Interested visitors register and enter your TeamMind group directly, becoming active community members

> Not a pop-up. Not a form. A real conversation that brings in users who actually want to stay.
