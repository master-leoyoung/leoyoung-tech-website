## FAQ

### Common Questions

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Go to Settings > Security > Change Password

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, use the export feature in Settings > Data Management

**Q: How many team members can I add?**
A: Depends on your subscription plan. Check our pricing page for details.
