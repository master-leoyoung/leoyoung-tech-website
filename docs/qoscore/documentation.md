## FAQ

### Common Questions

**Q: How do I create a custom scoring model?**
A: Go to Settings > Scoring Models > Create New Model and follow the wizard.

**Q: Can I integrate QoScore with my CI/CD pipeline?**
A: Yes, use our API or SDK to integrate with popular CI/CD tools.

**Q: What scoring methodologies are supported?**
A: QoScore supports various methodologies including weighted scoring, AHP, and custom algorithms.

**Q: How often should I run quality assessments?**
A: This depends on your industry and requirements. Common frequencies include daily, weekly, or per release cycle.
