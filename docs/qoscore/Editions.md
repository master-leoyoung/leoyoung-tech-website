# Community vs. Enterprise

<br>

---

**QoScore Platform Edition Comparison**

QoScore Platform is available in two editions to fit different use cases and 
organizational requirements. The Community Edition is built for quick adoption 
and foundational quality assessment. The Enterprise Edition is designed for 
organizations that require full-spectrum AI service governance — including 
safety and business risk coverage.

> For a complete breakdown of the three evaluation dimensions and 12 assessment > indicators, refer to the [QoScore Framework Documentation →](/qoscore/Framework)

## At a Glance

|                           | 🟢 Community Edition                   | 🔵 Enterprise Edition                          |
| ------------------------- | -------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **Evaluation Dimensions** | Quality dimension (partial indicators) | All three dimensions, 12 indicators            |
| **Safety Assessment**     | ❌ Not included                         | ✅ Fundamental Safety + Business Security       |
| **Deployment Mode**       | Black-box (user-side Input/Output)     | Black-box + Gray-box (LLM-API log integration) |
| **Customization**         | ❌                                      | ✅ UI, indicator tuning, private deployment     |
| **Support**               | Community (GitHub Issues)              | Email support during business hours (Mon–Fri)  |
| **License**               | Open source, free                      | Commercial license, contact for pricing        |

## Community Edition

**Free & Open Source**

The Community Edition operates on a black-box evaluation principle — it 
connects to user-side Input and Output data to perform lightweight AI service 
quality assessment, requiring no access to internal LLM components or APIs.

**Supported deployment targets:**

- RAG Agents
- Workflow Agents
- Self-hosted (private deployment) LLMs

**Not included in this edition:**

- Fundamental Safety assessment
- Business Security risk assessment

[View on GitHub →](https://github.com/leoyoungtech/qoscore)

## Enterprise Edition

**Full-Spectrum AI Service Governance**

The Enterprise Edition covers all three evaluation dimensions and all 12 
indicators defined in the QoScore Framework. Business Security risk assessment 
— evaluating the safety of AI outputs within real business contexts — is a 
defining capability of this edition, and a critical tool for organizations 
deploying AI at scale.

**Evaluation coverage:**

- ✅ All three dimensions, 12 assessment indicators
- ✅ Fundamental Safety: boundary-level safety evaluation
- ✅ Business Security: AI output risk assessment within business-specific contexts

**Deployment modes:**

- ✅ Black-box: user-side Input / Output evaluation
- ✅ Gray-box: LLM-API business log integration for deeper assessment insight

**Customization & Professional Services:**

- ✅ UI customization
- ✅ Indicator tuning (custom weights and thresholds per business scenario)
- ✅ Private deployment configuration support

**Support:**

- ✅ Email support during standard business hours (Mon–Fri, 9:00–18:00)

**Supported deployment targets:**

- RAG Agents
- Workflow Agents
- Tool-Calling Agents
- Multi-Agent Systems
- Self-hosted (private deployment) LLMs
